Privacy Policy

Inrium · Last updated: April 2026

Inrium is an app for managing Oral Anticoagulant Therapy (OAT/Sintrom). The privacy of your medical data is our absolute priority.

Data we collect

Inrium does not collect any personal data. All data you enter in the app (doses, INR measurements, notes, imported documents) is stored exclusively:

  • On your device (local storage).
  • In your private iCloud (if you have iCloud enabled), within your account's private database. Lappsus has no access at any time.

Health data

Inrium does not send any health data to any external server. Clinical data (doses, INR values, medical notes, PDF documents or TAO images) never leave your private iCloud.

If you enable the optional Apple Health integration, data is transferred directly between Inrium and the Health app on your device, with no intermediaries.

Analytics

Inrium may collect fully anonymous usage events (e.g. "document imported", "reminder activated"). These events contain no medical or personally identifiable data and are used solely to improve the app.

Third-party services

Inrium does not integrate any advertising, tracking, or social media services. The only third-party service is Apple iCloud, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Subscription (Inrium+)

Purchases are managed entirely through the Apple App Store (StoreKit). Lappsus does not store or process any payment data.

Children

Inrium does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for adult patients on anticoagulant therapy.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.
